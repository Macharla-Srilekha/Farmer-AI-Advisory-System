MSP Information

What is MSP?

Minimum Support Price (MSP) is the minimum price set by the government to protect farmers against any sharp fall in prices during bumper harvest years.

Where to Sell?

Sell at nearest APMC mandi or use eNAM platform for online trading across states. Keep all sale receipts for records.
